The 2026 Vision

A Theme Inspired by Tagore's Brushstrokes

Conceptualized by Avijit Ghatak

For 2026, Dutta Bagan AdhibasiBrinda proudly presents a visionary Durga Puja concept led by Avijit Ghatak. This year's theme draws profound inspiration from the artistic legacy of Rabindranath Tagore, particularly his distinctive paintings that embody emotion, abstraction, and depth. Blending heritage with contemporary interpretation, the concept aims to create an immersive cultural narrative that bridges tradition and modern aesthetics. Through innovative design, thoughtful storytelling, and refined execution, the project aspires to redefine thematic Durga Puja, offering audiences a unique, intellectually engaging, and visually compelling experience.
