ABOUT US

Where tradition meets celebration, and Puja becomes an emotion.

Born from pure devotion and community spirit, Dutta Bagan Adhibashibrinda brings fresh energy to timeless traditions. We are a young committee, but our celebration carries the weight and reverence of generations. More than just a festival, our Puja is a symbol of unity and joy. With Maa Durga's blessings, we are building a new legacy of celebration and service.
