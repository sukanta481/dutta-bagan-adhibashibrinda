Our Heritage

About Dutta Bagan

Dutta Bagan Adhibashibrinda has been at the heart of cultural celebrations for over half a century. What began as a small community gathering has blossomed into one of the most vibrant and anticipated festivities in the region, attracting devotees and visitors from across the nation.

Our roots run deep in tradition, yet our spirit embraces the energy of each new generation. The committee, composed of passionate local residents, works tirelessly to create an experience that honors our heritage while evolving with contemporary expression.

Our Vision

Looking ahead, we envision Dutta Bagan Adhibashibrinda as more than a festival ground—it is a living testament to community strength, cultural preservation, and the unifying power of faith. We aim to expand our reach while maintaining the intimate, heartfelt connection that defines our celebrations, ensuring that future generations inherit a tradition as vibrant and meaningful as the one we cherish today.
